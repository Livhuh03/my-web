About LYEF

Founded in 2018, LYEF is a community-based non-profit in Limpopo supporting unemployed youth through skills training, mentorship, and entrepreneurship programs.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To equip young people with skills that improve employability and self-reliance.

Vision: A generation of empowered youth driving economic and social change in Limpopo.
